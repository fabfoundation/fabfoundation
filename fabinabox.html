Open Design

We’re preparing to open source the Fab in a Box design—hardware files, build notes, and example curricula—so schools and community labs can adapt and improve the kit. Stay tuned for repo links and contribution guidelines as we finalize documentation.


If you want to contribute to the design now, don't hesitate to reach out, and if you would like to add your machine(s) to the setup (with a dedicated bracket), let us know!
